Department

Mechanical Engineering

Overview

The Department of Mechanical Engineering was started in the year 1996. The Department offers Undergraduate Programme (B.E) in Mechanical Engineering and Postgraduate Programme (M.E.) in Industrial Safety Engineering and Ph.D. programmes. The Department has highly qualified faculty members, out of those, one third are Ph.D. qualified. The Department obtained a funding of Rs. 1.20 Crores from various government agencies for research and development. The Department aims to develop Centers of Excellence in Industrial Pumps in collaboration with Aquasub Pumps, Coimbatore and Product Development Research Centre by Harita Tech Serve Ltd., Chennai..

Vision

Seek excellence in the field of Mechanical Engineering education through knowledge and skills to cater to the requirements of the society.

Mission

  1. To continuously improving the teaching and learning process to enable students to meet global needs.
  2. To upgrade the knowledge and skills of students, members of faculty and supporting staff through regular training.
  3. To produce the best minds of engineers capable of meeting expectations of Industry, Society and Entrepreneurship development.
